Buffalo Chicken Dip

This stuff is incredible! So good

Ingredients

Quantity Ingredient
1Cup Canned Chicken
8oz Buffalo Sause
8oz Cream Cheese
2Cup Shredded Cheddar Cheese

Instructions

  1. Mix everything together
  2. Place in a 13x9pan
  3. Bake at 350 degrees
  4. Enjoy
